Positioning & messaging (general sketch)

Positioning statement (draft template)

Kvasir is a platform for creators to publish structured learning and stories, and for audiences to discover, follow, and return. It combines creator tools, distribution, and community so creators can grow and earn.

Primary value props

  • For creators: publish quickly, organize content into “series/courses”, reach an audience, get feedback, monetize.
  • For consumers: discover trustworthy niche content, follow creators, save progress, get recommendations.

Proof points to build (not copy yet)

  • Creator outcomes: views, follows, subscriptions, donations, retention
  • Content quality: curation, moderation, clear rules
  • Product mechanics: “continue”, bookmarks, notifications, sharing, contests/events

Message pillars (examples)

  • Structured: not just posts — content is organized into components/series.
  • Discoverable: built for search and sharing; good internal linking and topic pages.
  • Creator-first: distribution and monetization are first-class, not afterthoughts.
  • Community: feedback loops, events, contests, and ongoing engagement.

What to avoid early

  • Too many features in the headline (keep it simple).
  • Unverifiable claims (“best”, “#1”) without evidence.
  • Generic positioning that overlaps with every platform.
