Content, community, and partnerships (general sketch)

Content strategy (owned)

  • Evergreen: guides, roadmaps, glossaries in chosen niches (feeds SEO and shareability).
  • Creator stories: “how I teach X”, “how I built my series”, outcomes and process.
  • Product education: onboarding docs and publishing best-practices.

Community strategy

Creator community (core)

  • private space for early creators: feedback, feature requests, mutual promotion
  • regular office hours; fast support; share “what works” playbooks

Public community (optional, later)

  • open groups around niches once content density is high
  • moderation and clear rules from day one

Partnerships (practical early list)

  • Newsletters: niche curators who can feature a topic page or creator.
  • Podcasts: creator interviews, teaching niches, learning productivity.
  • Communities: Discords, subreddits, forums (value-first participation).
  • Tools: creator tools (writing, video, productivity) for co-marketing.

Partnership offer (simple)

  • Curated “best of” pages for partner audiences
  • Guest posts / joint webinars
  • Creator bundles or challenges (publish-week event)

Rule: lead with audience value; avoid pay-to-play until tracking works.

Events & contests

  • monthly themed challenge (publish 3 lessons in 7 days)
  • contest tied to niches; winners highlighted in platform + email + social
  • live sessions: “creator teardown”, “topic roadmap”
