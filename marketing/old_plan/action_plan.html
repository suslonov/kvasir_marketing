Startup Marketing Action Plan

Constraint: No budget. 1-2 people. ~10 hrs/week max for marketing.

1. Google SEO (Do Once, Then Maintain)

This Week:
  1. Go to Google Search Console → Add quizly.pub
  2. Verify via DNS TXT record
  3. Submit sitemap: https://quizly.pub/sitemap.xml
  4. URL Inspection → Request indexing for:
    • /deck
    • /contest?id=1, /contest?id=2, /contest?id=3
Monthly: Check GSC for errors. Add new pages to sitemap when created.

2. Get First Users (Pick ONE Channel)

Channel Best If... Weekly Time
Reddit You can write helpful answers in learning/AI subreddits 3 hrs
Twitter/X You enjoy short-form, can post demos/threads 2 hrs
Discord servers Target audience hangs out in specific communities 3 hrs
LinkedIn B2B angle, targeting professionals 2 hrs
Pick ONE. Be helpful first (2-3 weeks). Then share your stuff sparingly.

3. Content That Works (Minimum Viable)

  • 1 demo video (30-60 sec) showing the product in action → pin everywhere
  • 1 landing page that explains value in 10 seconds → /deck already exists
  • 3-5 example chats/games people can try immediately

Don't need: blog, newsletter, podcast, content calendar (yet)

4. Viral Mechanics (Build Into Product)

  • Share button on every chat/game with pre-filled text
  • Result cards — shareable image when user completes something
  • "Created on Quizly" watermark/link on shared content

5. Free Backlinks (When You Have Time)

  • HARO (helpareporter.com) — respond to journalist queries about AI/education. 15 min/day.
  • Product Hunt — launch when you have 5+ working demos
  • Hacker News "Show HN" — post when product is interesting enough

6. Weekly Routine (2-3 hrs total)

  • Mon: 30 min — check GSC for errors, respond to any user feedback
  • Tue-Thu: 1 hr — engage in your ONE chosen community
  • Fri: 30 min — post 1 thing (demo, update, insight)

7. What NOT To Do

  • ❌ Pay influencers
  • ❌ Run ads before product-market fit
  • ❌ Be on 5 social platforms at once
  • ❌ Write long blog posts nobody reads
  • ❌ Build complex email automation
  • ❌ Spend time on "branding"

8. Success Metrics (Keep It Simple)

Metric Where Target
Signups/week Your DB Growing
Games played Your analytics Growing
Google impressions GSC Any > 0
