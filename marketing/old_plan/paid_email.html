Paid growth + email/CRM (general sketch)

Paid growth (when to start)

  • Only after conversion tracking is reliable and landing pages are stable.
  • Start with retargeting (site visitors, engaged users) before cold acquisition.
  • Use small tests to learn which niches/landing pages convert.

Paid channel ideas

  • Search ads: intent-heavy queries for a niche topic page.
  • Social ads: retargeting + lookalikes of activated users/creators.
  • Sponsorships: newsletter/podcast spots where tracking links are accepted.

Email/CRM (core early)

User lifecycle emails

  • Welcome: what to do next (follow creators, pick interests)
  • Digest: weekly “new in your interests”
  • Continue: reminders to resume started series/courses

Creator lifecycle emails

  • Onboarding: publish-first checklist + templates
  • Activation: prompts to publish the next item / complete a series
  • Outcomes: weekly summary (views/follows/saves) + suggestions

Segments (keep it simple)

  • new users (0–7 days), returning users, inactive users
  • creators: published 0 / 1–2 / 3+ items; “first win” achieved or not
  • interest tags/niches

Success metrics

  • Email: open/click rates are secondary; primary is activation/retention lift.
  • Paid: CAC proxy, payback proxy (or engagement payback), cohort retention.
